Our Mission

We're trying to build a future where diseases are a thing of the past.

The Problem

Our current state of healthcare is reactive. You only know the current state of your health after a doctor diagnoses you, meaning your chance of survivial depends on when you go to the doctor, instead of you having the ability to control your own health.

Read More

The Solution

Introducing Iris, a platform where numerous sensors and wearables record biomarkers from testosterone levels, to how stressed you are every day. This data is then analyzed by leading AI algorithms where actionable insights and recommendaitons are created for you.

Read More

The Outcome

A world where you aren't afraid of the next doctor's visit. You are in control of your own health, receiving the best insights and recommendaitons to prevent any diseases from happening, transforming healthcare from being reactive, to preventative.

Read More
